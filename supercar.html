British cars are famous for being recognised as the most luxurious in the world, but some car manufacturers are turning to sports cars to compete with other European supercars, and here are some examples...

McLaren MP4

To return to the production of the F1-based McLaren we cannot fail to mention the MP4, the car presented for the 1998 season with which the drivers Ayrton Senna and Alain Prost won 15 of the 16 GPs.

DeLorean DMC-12

As for Lotus, in 1980, he agreed with DeLorean for the realization of the DMC-12, this car produced in 9000 units is famous for its "gullwing" doors, this car was the inspiration for the film " Back to the Future".

Naran GT 3

The market for supercars is always looking to increase, Naran Automotive is a new manufacturer of sports cars, this photo is one of only 49 examples that will be produced with a price of about a million, being a supercar has a V8 engine 5.0 biturbo, with 1036 horses can get a top speed of 370 km/h.

Aston Martin Valhalla

The Aston Martin Valhalla is an exclusive project that will see production of around 500 units per year, starting in 2023, which should be priced at around 750-800,000 €. It is equipped with a petrol-electric hybrid system that delivers a total of 950 HP and 1,000 Nm of torque. The V6, which the British company wanted to develop in-house, has been shelved in favour of a twin-turbo V8 with a power output of 950 HP.
