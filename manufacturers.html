Let's discover together some of the most famous and popular British car manufacturers.

City Cars

The Mini is a British car manufacturer (subsidiary of the German BMW group since the mid 90s) The Mini is a small car suitable for city life with 3 or 5 doors with the optional convertible. There are also the "Cooper SE" (full electric) and "Cooper S" models for lovers of sport driving where around 180 HP are available on a 1300 kg car.

Sport Cars

As for sports cars produced by British car manufacturers, the best known brands are Jaguar, Lotus and McLaren.
"Jaguar Land Rover" is a British automotive multinational based in Coventry, West Midlands, in 2008 it was bought by Tata Motors together with Ford.
Lotus is headquartered in a former air base near Norwich, following the financial exposures due to the agreement entered into with the De Lorean Motor Company for the construction of the DMC-12, in 1986 l company was acquired by General Motors.
"McLaren Automotive" began producing the Mercedes-Benz SLR McLaren on behalf of Mercedes-Benz, later in 2010 the company declared itself independent and the following year began producing the McLaren MP4-12C, starting a road production as well as that for F1.

Luxury Cars

Aston Martin is a British car manufacturer born in the early 1900s with the name "Bamford & Martin" then changed to "Aston Martin Ltd" and finally to "Aston Martin Lagonda Limited". The small company produces Gran Turismo made with artisanal care.
The Bentley is the main supplier of the British family and has won the famous "24 Hours of Le Mans" 6 times. The Bentley was bought by the competitor Rolls Royce who made it his sub-brand for about 60 years, starting from '98 it is part of the Volkswagen group.
The "Rolls Royce Motor Cars Limited" has always been Bentley's main rival, with the new millennium the Rolls Royce brand became part of the BMW group.

Land Rover Vs Range Rover

You have probably already heard of Land Rover and Rage Rover ... contrary to what many think, these 2 types of SUVs are part of the same car manufacturer: the "Land Rover". A "Range Rover" is nothing more than a specific model of "Land Rover" where its full name would be "Land Rover Range Rover", just like the name "Mustang" is short for "Ford Mustang".
Range Rover is also the name of a Land Rover sub-category consisting of 4 unique SUVs (Range Rover, Range Rover Sport, Range Rover Velar and Range Rover Evoque).
